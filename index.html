Welcome

We are the Mountain View Ultimate Frisbee Club. We compete in the Boise Ultimate Scene Youth League against other teams in the league. We practice Tuesdays and Thursdays out in the front practice fields at Mountain View High School. Games are held on Mondays at Optamist Fields at 4:30(PM) and 5:45(PM). New members are always allowed to join and we'd love to see you out there with us

Sign up

Intrested in playing for Mountain View? Or maybe you want to play for another team in the league? You can sign up at the Boise Ultimate Scene Youth Website.
