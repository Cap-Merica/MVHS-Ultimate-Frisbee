Who are We?

We are MVHS Ultimate and we play in the Boise Ultimate Scene Youth (BUSY) league. We are new to the league but we intend to make our mark in our first season. If you have any questions for us check out the Contact page. Rules for Ultimate Frisbee can be found on the rules page

When do we Start?

Our Season starts on March 1st with practices being held every Tuesday and Thursday in front of Mountain View from 3:00(PM) - 4:30(PM). The games are held at Optomist Fields where double headers are held. PLAYERS MUST BE AT THE FIELD AT 4:00! First game is from 4:30(PM) - 5:45(PM) and the second game is from 6:00(PM) - 7:15(PM). The last game of the offical season ends on April 29th and the Tournament will begin shortly after.
